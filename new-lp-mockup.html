Frequently asked questions

Is RV Weather an app?+
No, it's a website you can use from any phone, tablet or computer. Nothing to install.
How is SureRoute different from a weather app?+
Weather apps show you conditions. SureRoute translates those conditions into RV-specific driving impact, then uses them plus your preferences to calculate a safer, more enjoyable route.
How is it different from my GPS?+
It's a planning tool, not a turn-by-turn tracker. Our road database is scrubbed of roads unsuitable for RVs and accounts for your vehicle size and weight. Use your GPS for directions; use SureRoute to choose the route and day.
Do I have to pay for a whole year?+
No. Start with the $0.99 7-day trial, then choose monthly or annual. You can pause a monthly subscription when you're not traveling and only pay for the months you're on the road.
Can I plan trips in Canada and Mexico?+
Yes. From Edmonton and the Canadian Maritimes down to Baja Mexico, we've got you covered.
